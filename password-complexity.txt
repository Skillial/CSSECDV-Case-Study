Password Complexity Rules
For a password to be considered valid, it must meet all of these criteria:
Minimum Length: It must be at least 8 characters long.
Uppercase Letter: It must contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z).
Lowercase Letter: It must contain at least one lowercase letter (a-z).
Number: It must contain at least one number (0-9).
Special Character: It must contain at least one special character from the following set: !@#$%^&*
